About Me

I am currently in the middle of completing a full stack software engineering course through Codecademy. I was recently introduced to web development by husband who is a full stack engineer and enjoyed the challenge that coding gave me.


I am a working of mom of two, and a house full of pets. I have a degree in Psychology and I am a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist. For the past almost decade, I provided therapy to indviduals, families, couples, and groups. I have taught various psychology courses to undergraduates and I have consulted with NYC schools on various mental health and social emotional topics. In all honesty, to be there for someone in an emotional and mental way can be very taxing and I no longer had the capacity to give as I once did. Software development has brought out another passion and I truly look forward to continuing my journey down this path.


When I am not studying or coding, I am running after two beautiful girls who are the reasons I have found my voice and for who I get up everyday. I enjoy my daily dog walks, as we chase squirrels and my morning wake up meows from a hungry cat. I enjoy indoor cycling, creating things in the kitchen, wine, food, and hosting events. Also, I am a true fan of the snow and might be slightly addicted to coffee.
